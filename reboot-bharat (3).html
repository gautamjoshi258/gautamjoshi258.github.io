Why This Matters

Two problems. One solution.

India generates millions of tons of electronic waste every year. At the same time, crores of students — especially in Jaipur, Nagaur, and rural Rajasthan — have never touched a computer. We connect both sides of this equation.

Bridging the Digital Divide

Students in Nagaur and rural Rajasthan deserve the same digital access as anyone in a metro city. We are working to close that gap one device at a time, across India.

Powering Future Careers

A laptop or PC is a young person's gateway — to coding, design, communication, and real opportunity. Without access, millions are left behind before they even begin.

Reducing Electronic Waste

Old laptops and PCs sitting in offices and homes are a resource, not rubbish. Refurbishing them keeps harmful materials out of landfills and creates lasting value for students.

FAQ

Common questions.

What devices do you accept?
We accept laptops and desktop PCs of all brands and models in any condition — working, partially working, or completely broken. Even if a device cannot be repaired, it will be responsibly dismantled rather than dumped.
Can I donate more than one device?
Absolutely — the more the better. If you have two, three, or more unused laptops or PCs at home or in your office, every single one can reach a student. Even donating just one from a set makes a real difference.
Is my data safe when I donate?
Yes. Every device goes through DoD-standard (Department of Defense) data erasure — a multi-pass overwrite that makes all data completely unrecoverable. We can provide a data destruction confirmation on request.
How is pickup arranged and is it really free?
Pickup is completely free. After you submit the donation form, we will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a convenient time. We collect from anywhere in India. For bulk donations (10+ devices), we usually arrange same-day collection.
Where do the devices go?
Devices go directly to students in our partner government schools in Jaipur and Nagaur, Rajasthan. We are adding more schools across Rajasthan. You will receive a confirmation once your device reaches a student.
How can I volunteer?
We welcome volunteers for device collection, basic repairs, school visits, and social media outreach. If you are based in Jaipur or Nagaur, we especially need on-ground help. Fill the contact form below or email us directly at rebootbharat@gmail.com.
